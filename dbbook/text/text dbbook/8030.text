Riddle of the Seven Realms is a fantasy novel by Lyndon Hardy, first published in 1988. It is the third of a trilogy set in the same world; the first book is Master of the Five Magics and the second Secret of the Sixth Magic. While the books feature different characters, each explores, in successively more detail, the same system of magic.
