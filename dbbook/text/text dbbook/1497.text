Sour Sweet is a novel by Timothy Mo first published in 1982. Written as a 'sour sweet' comedy the story follows the tribulations of a Hong Kong Chinese immigrant and his initially reluctant wife as they attempt to make a home for themselves in 1960s [1] London. [2]
Film adaptation
The novel was adaptated for the screen in 1988. Mike Newell directed. Novelist Ian McEwan wrote the script. [3]
