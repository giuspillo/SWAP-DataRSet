An April Shroud is a crime novel by Reginald Hill, the fourth novel in the Dalziel and Pascoe series. The novel is mainly Dalziel's, as Peter and Ellie Pascoe marry and go on honeymoon. Taking leave himself, Dalziel stumbles across a case of embezzlement and murder, which he solves with Pascoe's last minute assistance in the closing chapters.
