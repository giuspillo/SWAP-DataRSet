Mountain of Black Glass is the third book in Tad Williams' acclaimed Otherland Series. It was first published in 1999 with a paperback edition in 2000. Continuing from River of Blue Fire it brings the characters together at the battle of Troy and finally to the heart of the Grail Brotherhood.
