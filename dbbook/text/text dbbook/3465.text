The Far Side Gallery 3 is the third anthology of Gary Larson's The Far Side comic strips. Cartoons from previous collections The Far Side Observer, Night of the Crash-Test Dummies, and Wildlife Preserves are featured, all of which were printed from 1987-1989. The volume is dedicated to guitar maker Jimmy D'Aquisto, and the foreword was written by Stephen Jay Gould. The cover depicts a parody of Leonardo da Vinci's Mona Lisa, substituting a cow for the female subject.
