Five Are Together Again (published 1963) is a children's novel in The Famous Five series by Enid Blyton. First published by Hodder and Stoughton, and in its first edition illustrated by Eileen Soper. This is the 21st and last complete novel to feature the Famous Five and was published 21 years after their first adventure Five on a Treasure Island.
Five Are Together Again (published 1963) is a children's novel in The Famous Five series by Enid Blyton . First published by Hodder and Stoughton , and in its first edition illustrated by Eileen Soper .
This is the 21st and last complete novel to feature the Famous Five and was published 21 years after their first adventure Five on a Treasure Island .
Plot introduction
The children are supposed to be staying at Kirrin Cottage but as soon as George's mother and father's maid Joanna catches scarlet fever , the Five are sent to live with an old friend, called Tinker, and his famous scientist father. When Tinker's (who appeared in ' Five Go To Demon's Rocks ' (1961)) father's top secret papers go missing, it is left up to the children to find the thief, whoever he is. There are some circus folk camping in Tinker's field.
