Our Cancer Year is a graphic novel written by Harvey Pekar and Joyce Brabner and illustrated by Frank Stack .
Published in 1994 by the New York Press publisher Four Walls Eight Windows , Our Cancer Year (an offshoot of the cult favorite comic book series American Splendor ) relates the story of Harvey's struggle to overcome cancer , as well as serving as a social commentary on events of that year. Our Cancer Year won the 1995 Harvey Award for best original graphic novel . [1]
