Creatures of the Night is a book about the cult following of the motion picture The Rocky Horror Picture Show, written by Sal Piro. Piro has been President of the National Fan club since 1977. The book contains information about the beginnings of the cult following as well as Piro's own experiences as a fan of the movie.
