The Unusual Suspects is the second novel in The Sisters Grimm series written by Michael Buckley.

The Chelsea Review: (:P$4%5)
The Unusual Suspects is a wonderful, fascinating story about adventure, crime, and mystery! Living in a secret magical town with fairytale creatures and living side by side with humans. Sabrina and Daphne must work together, with the help of their grandmother and their 200 pound Great Dane to solve each injustice that they come to run across. They must face lots of challenges and come in contact with some evil villains, like Rumpelstilskin or The group of The Scarlet Hand.
