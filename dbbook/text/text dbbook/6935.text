Floating in My Mother's Palm is a 1990 novel by Ursula Hegi. The story centers on a young girl, Hannah Malter, growing up in post-war Germany and her family and many of their fellow townsfolk in 'Burgdorf', including Trudi Montag who also appears in Hegi's Stones from the River.
