The Snow Leopard is a 1978 book by Peter Matthiessen . It is an account of his two-month search for the snow leopard with naturalist George Schaller in the Dolpo region on the Tibetan Plateau in the Himalayas .
Awards and acclaim
The Snow Leopard won the 1979 National Book Award in the category Contemporary Thought [1] and the 1980 National Book Award for Nonfiction (paperback). [2] It has garnered more critical acclaim since then. [3] It has been included in several lists of best travel books including World Hum's ten most celebrated books, [4] Washington Post Book World's Travel Books That Will Take You Far, [5] and National Geographic Traveler 's Around the World in 80+ Books. [6]
