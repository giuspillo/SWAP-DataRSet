Secret of the Sixth Magic is a fantasy novel by Lyndon Hardy, first published in 1984. It is the second of a trilogy set in the same world; the first book is Master of the Five Magics and the third Riddle of the Seven Realms. While the books feature different characters, each explores, in successively more detail, the same system of magic.
