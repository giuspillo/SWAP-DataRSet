Up In A Heaval is the twenty-sixth book of the Xanth series by Piers Anthony.
Plot introduction
An innocent piece of Mundane Snail Mail has provoked the dreaded Demon JU(P/I)ter to hurl his Red Spot at the magical land of Xanth. As the dire Dot draws closer, the unwelcome ordeal of saving the enchanted realm falls to Umlaut, an unlikely lad with an unknown past and an uncertain future.
