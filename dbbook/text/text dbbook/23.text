The Tail of Emily Windsnap (2003) is a children's fantasy novel written by Liz Kessler. The story is about a land-born half-mermaid who discovers her family history and explores around to find the truth.

The Emily Windsnap series is an ongoing series of children's fantasy novels written by British author Liz Kessler . The first novel, The Tail of Emily Windsnap, was published in 2003 through Candlewick Press , who has published the entirety of the series to date. The series first began as a poem that Kessler was writing about a "little girl who lived on a boat but had a big secret". [1] An editor recommended that Kessler turn the poem into a book. [1]
