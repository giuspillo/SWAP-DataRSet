Dream Jungle is a novel by Jessica Hagedorn , a Filipino American author.
The book was published in 2003 by Penguin Viking press. [1] [2]
Description
The book is set in Philippines of the 1970s. The plot revolves around the purported discovery of a fictionalized stone-age "lost tribe" by a rich, privileged Westerner .
