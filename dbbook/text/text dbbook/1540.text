Vinyl Cafe Diaries (2003) is Stuart McLean's fourth volume of stories that first aired on the CBC Radio program The Vinyl Cafe. It made the 2004 Stephen Leacock Award for Humour shortlist, and was the winner of the 2004 Canadian Authors Association Award. Stories included in Vinyl Cafe Diaries: Walking Man Dave and the Duck Tree of Heaven Lazy Lips Labour Pains Birthday Present Rashida, Amir and the Great Gift-Giving Book Club A Night to Remember Dorm Days Best Things Christmas on the Road Field Trip No Tax on Truffles Gifted Planet Boy
