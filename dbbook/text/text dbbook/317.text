Bloodchild and Other Stories {{#invoke:InfoboxImage|InfoboxImage|image=Image:Bloodchild. gif|size=|sizedefault=frameless|alt=|border=|suppressplaceholder=yes}}Author(s) Octavia E. ButlerCountry 22x20px United StatesGenre(s) science fiction, horrorPublisher Four Walls Eight Windows, Seven Stories PressPublication date Aug/Sep 1995Media type PrintPages 144 ppISBN ISBN 1-56858-055-X (1st ed. ) Bloodchild and Other Stories is the only and rather brief collection of science fiction stories and essays by Octavia E. Butler. Each story features an afterword by Butler. "Bloodchild", the title story, won both Hugo Award and Nebula Award.
