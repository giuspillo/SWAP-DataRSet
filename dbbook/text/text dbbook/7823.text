Waldo & Magic, Inc. is a book containing those two novellas, one science fiction, one fantasy, by Robert A. Heinlein. It was published in 1950. Contents: "Waldo" (1942; originally published in Astounding Science Fiction) "Magic, Inc. " (1940; originally published in Unknown)
