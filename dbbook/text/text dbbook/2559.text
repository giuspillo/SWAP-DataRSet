The First Three Minutes: A Modern View of the Origin of the Universe is a book written by Steven Weinberg and published in 1977. It attempts to explain the early stages of the Universe after the Big Bang: from Very Early Universe (Planck epoch ending 10 seconds after the Big Bang) to the Early Universe (starting of the Photon epoch, from 10 seconds after the Big Bang and over).
