The Singing Sword is a historical fiction novel written by Jack Whyte , first published in 1993. It is the second novel in " A Dream of Eagles " series.Publisher's Weekly described Whyte's approach to historical fiction as a "dirt-beneath-the nails version of the Arthurian ""Camulod""" and praised it as "a top-notch Arthurian tale forged to a sharp edge in the fires of historical realism" [1]
