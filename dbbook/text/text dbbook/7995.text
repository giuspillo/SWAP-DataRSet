Chocolate Chip Cookie Murder is a 2000 mystery novel by Joanne Fluke and the first novel in the Hannah Swensen Mysteries series. The book was first published on March 1, 2000 in hardback through Kensington Books.

Joanne Fluke (born c. 1943, Swanville, Minnesota ) is a writer (Hannah Swensen Mysteries). She has written fifteen books published in the series. Her series has been known for its recipes, its comedy, and endings. Her books contain romance as well. She currently lives in southern California . Her works have been published under several pseudonyms, including Jo Gibson, Chris Hunter, John Fisher, Kathryn Kirkwood and Gina Jackson.[ citation needed ]
Fluke published 2 novellas , Candy Cane Murder and Gingerbread Cookie Murder. The 2007 and 2010 books include three stories; the other pieces were by Laura Levine and Leslie Meier.[ citation needed ]
