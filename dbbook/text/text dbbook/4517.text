Vinyl Cafe Unplugged (2000) is Stuart McLean's third volume of stories that first aired on the CBC Radio program The Vinyl Cafe. In 2001, it won the Stephen Leacock Award for Humour. This was the second time that Stuart McLean had won for his writings on The Vinyl Cafe. Stories included in Vinyl Cafe Unplugged: Arthur Galway The Fly Christmas Presents Harrison Ford's Toes Dorothy The Last Kind Word Blues The Bare Truth Susan is Serious Odd Jobs The Razor's Edge Morley's Christmas Pageant Figs Love Never Ends
