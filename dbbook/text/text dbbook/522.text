Red Kayak is a young adult novel by American author Priscilla Cummings. It was first published in 2004.
Synopsis
Brady loves his life of crabbing and oyster fishing on Chesapeake Bay with his friends J.T. Digger, and his mother and father. While Brady makes friends with new rich families moving into the area, also the DiAngelos, his parents and friends all become angry about the new construction. Unfortunately, a seemingly random kayak accident takes the life of the DiAngelo's son, a toddler. Brady tries to help Mrs. DiAngelo recover but finds out something interesting. Digger and J.T used a drill to cut and refill holes in the kayak. They had killed Ben. Because they did this horrible act, J.T and Digger were sent to a forestry camp for 9 months.
