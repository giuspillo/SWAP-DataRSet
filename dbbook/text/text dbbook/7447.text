Born Blue is a young adult novel by award winning author Han Nolan in 2001. Born Blue follows the life of Janie, a young adult trying to fulfill her dream to become a famous singer. It has many references to Billie Holiday, Ella Fitzgerald, Etta James, and many other famous singers whom she calls "The Ladies".
