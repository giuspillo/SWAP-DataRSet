Battlestations! is a Star Trek: The Original Series novel written by Diane Carey.
Plot
Lt. Commander Piper is taking a vacation from Star Fleet following the events of the novel Dreadnought! in which she prevented a military coup from taking over the Federation. However she is swept up into intrigue when Captain Kirk is arrested for the theft of transwarp drive, a new technology which could radically shift the balance of power across the galaxy. Piper, Commander Spock , and Dr. McCoy attempt to solve the mystery as the major powers of the galaxy scramble for the new technology.
