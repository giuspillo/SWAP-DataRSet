Eden* is a Japanese visual novel by Minori for the PC playable as a DVD and was released on September 18, 2009 with a rating for everyone. An updated adult patch of the game entitled Eden* Plus+Mosaic was released on the same day. Other games by Minori include Wind: A Breath of Heart and Ef: A Fairy Tale of the Two.. A manga adaptation illustrated by Takeshi Moriki began serialization in the August 2009 issue of Ichijinsha's Comic Rex manga magazine.
