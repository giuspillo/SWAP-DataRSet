The Known World is a 2003 historical novel by Edward P. Jones. Set in antebellum Virginia, it examines the issues regarding the ownership of black slaves by both white and black Americans. A complex text with a wide-ranging scope, The Known World paints an enormous canvas thick with personalities and situations that show the devastating effects of slavery, whilst also offering hope of how this heinous institution can be transcended.
