The Scheme for Full Employment is a novel by the English author Magnus Mills, published in 2003 by Flamingo.
Plot introduction
The scheme referred to in the title involves the driving of 'UniVans' from depot to depot picking up and unloading cargo - the cargo being replacement parts for UniVans.
