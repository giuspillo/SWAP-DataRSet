Among the Hidden is a 1998 young adult novel by Margaret Peterson Haddix concerning a fictional future in which overly drastic measures have been taken to quell overpopulation . It is the first of seven novels in the Shadow Children series.
Following the life of third child Luke Garner as he asses his life as one of the "shadow children", forced to hide in the shadows as he hides himself from the Population Police who kill any child born after their first two siblings due to overpopulation. Luke, feeling alone and isolated soon finds comfort with another shadow child Jenny in the Baron household across from him with whom he finds ways to communicate with. Through Jenny's influence Luke finds his confidence and courage to take down the Population Police in several books.
