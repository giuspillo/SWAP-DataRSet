Night Chills is a suspense novel by best-selling author Dean Koontz originally published in 1976. The novel concerns a plot by an emotionally disturbed scientist, a corrupt U.S. Army officer, and a wealthy businessman to brainwash the residents of an isolated New England town through techniques related to subliminal advertising.
