L'Arena is an Italian local daily newspaper, based in San Martino Buon Albergo, Italy. Founded in 1866, shortly before the annexation of Veneto into the Kingdom of Italy, L'Arena is one of the oldest newspapers in Italy and the most popular newspaper in Verona.
