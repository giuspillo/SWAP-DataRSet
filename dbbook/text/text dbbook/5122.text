Darcy's Story by Janet Aylmer was one of the first novels published after the success of the BBC One serial of Jane Austen's Pride and Prejudice in 1995. Published in England in 1996, the novel tells the story from Mr. Darcy's point of view. In 2006, the novel was published by HarperCollins in the USA. Author Janet Aylmer has since published a sequel, Dialogue with Darcy, on Kindle.
